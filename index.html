Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What kind of images work best?

The AI is optimized for images with clear subjects and defined outlines, such as product photos, illustrations, and portraits.

Is my image data secure?

Yes, all uploaded images are processed securely and are not stored after the line art generation is complete.

Can I use the generated line art commercially?

Yes, you retain full commercial rights to all line art generated using EdgeWizard.
