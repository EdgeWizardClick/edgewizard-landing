How It Works

EdgeWizard is designed to be simple — One-Click to clean, precise outlines.

  • Upload your image or use the camera
  • Choose your line style
    Select Thin Lines or Bold Lines.
  • Add optional extras
    Keep Black Lines or add an Outer Border.
  • Press the click button
    High-precision edge detection starts automatically.
  • Download your result
    Get clean line art as a PNG, perfect outlines and coloring pages.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ )

What kind of images work best? +

EdgeWizard works best with images that have clear subjects and defined outlines, such as illustrations, clipart and high-contrast photos.

Is my image data secure? +

Yes. All uploaded images are processed securely and are not stored after the line art generation is complete.

Can I use the generated line art commercially? +

Yes. You retain full commercial rights to all line art generated using EdgeWizard.

How long does the processing take? +

High-precision processing may take a few seconds depending on image size.

Do I need to create an account? +

Yes. An account is required to use EdgeWizard. Creating an account is free and gives you daily free credits.
